Rental Solutions

Al Salem Johnson Controls is a cooling industry expert with a complete line of chiller, and air conditioner/air handler rentals, which are the best solutions when a chiller goes off-line for maintenance or your system suddenly goes down, or when additional or backup equipment is necessary to keep operations running.

Whether a chiller goes off-line for maintenance or your system suddenly goes down, Al Salem Johnson Controls has a full range of YORK products & emergency protocol to handle any situation in a fast, efficient & proactive manner.

With depots located throughout Saudi Arabia, we can immediately respond to your HVAC needs and provide an entire rental solution quickly and completely. Our experts will work with you to determine what equipment will work best and provide a turnkey solution which includes:

  • Initial diagnosis of situation
  • Commissioning of equipment
  • Operation & maintenance of equipment during rental period
  • Decommissioning and removal of equipment
